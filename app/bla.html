Pushh is a free iPhone app that helps you keep track of your fitness selfies.

App Store

Snap

Match your photos

Use Pushh’s super easy grid lines or toggle on your last photo as an transparent overlay as you’re taking a photo. The composition of your new photos will match up to your older photos.

Log your measurements

Log your body measurements so you’ll be able to build a narrative around your before-and-after. Besides body fat percentage Pushh lets you enter metric (kg) or imperial (lbs) units.

Organize

A glance at your progress

All of your fitness selfies are automagically laid out in a nice timeline style. If you’ve taken more than one selfie within a certain time frame they’ll group together.

Import from your iPhone

Started your transformation journey before you were using Pushh? No worries, just import them from your iPhone and Pushh will put them in chronological order.

Export & Share

Easily export your progress

On the road to your ultimate fitness transformation, export a single fitness selfie or multiple fitness selfies at any time.

Beautiful before-and-after's

As soon as you’re ready to eternalize your progress, select the photos you want a comparison of and create a beautiful image or movie.

Share your results

Show off your body transformation you worked so hard for to achieve to your friends on Facebook.

Privacy

Some people rather keep their fitness progress to private. Pushh lets you set a passcode to make sure only you can get to your fitness selfies in the app.

Reminders

Tracking your progress starts with taking pictures periodically. Set daily, weekly or monthly reminders to get reminded to take a selfie.
